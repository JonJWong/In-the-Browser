JS the Groove

This is a 4-arrow rhythm game inspired by Stepmania

ITG with the Simply Love theme, coded in Javascript

How to play:

In this game, move your feet and dance to the beat. This is a fun and interactive rhythm game where the goal is to hit the arrows when they reach the targets at the top of the screen.

The receptors are controlled by the arrow keys, and depending on how well-timed you press them when the notes come up, you will be awarded points based on your timing.

If you miss an arrow, or hit a mine, your health bar will decrease, so be careful Hitting arrows will gradually increase your health, If your health decreases to 0 You lose!
